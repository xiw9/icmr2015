ACM ICMR 2015

June 23-26, Shanghai, China

Shanghai International Convention Center

2727 Binjiang Ave, Pudong, Shanghai

Introduction

The Annual ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval (ICMR) offers a great opportunity for exchanging leading-edge multimedia retrieval ideas among researchers, practitioners and other potential users of multimedia retrieval systems.

Program

The ICMR 2015 will be held between June 23rd 2015 and June 26th 2015 at the Shanghai International Convention Center.
